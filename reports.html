Organizing the Government Affairs Function for Impact

Date reviewed: July 4, 2026

Source: McKinsey report

Purpose of the Report

This report explains how government affairs functions can be organized to create more strategic impact, improve coordination, and connect policy work more directly to business outcomes.

Key Takeaways

  • Government affairs should not operate only as a reactive function.
  • The function is more valuable when it connects policy risk, stakeholder intelligence, business planning, and executive decision-making.
  • Clear ownership, issue prioritization, and internal coordination are essential.
